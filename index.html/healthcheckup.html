What's Included in Full Body Checkup?

The Full Body Checkup (Executive Package) is the most comprehensive health assessment package available. It combines all premium blood tests with advanced imaging and specialized clinical examination. This complete package provides a thorough health evaluation of your entire body.

🩸 All Premium Blood Tests Included

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Thyroid Profile (TSH, T3, T4)
  • Liver Function Tests

  • Lipid Profile (Cholesterol)
  • Diabetes Screening (Glucose, HbA1C)

📊 Advanced Imaging Tests Included

  • ECG (Electrocardiogram) - Heart function analysis

  • Abdominal Ultrasound - Organ visualization

  • Comprehensive Health Analysis
  • Personalized Health Recommendations

Test Breakdown by Category

Blood Tests (6 major categories)

  • Complete Blood Count - detects infections, anemia, blood disorders
  • Thyroid Tests - checks metabolism and thyroid function
  • Liver Tests - evaluates liver health and function
  • Kidney Tests - assesses kidney function and health
  • Lipid Tests - measures cholesterol and heart disease risk
  • Diabetes Tests - screens for diabetes and glucose issues

Imaging Tests (3 advanced imaging)

  • ECG - Records heart electrical activity and detects arrhythmias
  • Chest X-Ray - Visualizes lungs, heart size, and chest structures
  • Abdominal Ultrasound - Images liver, kidneys, pancreas, spleen, gallbladder

Preparation Guidelines

Before Your Test

  • 8-10 hours fasting (for accurate blood work)
  • Wear loose-sleeved comfortable clothing
  • Stay hydrated with water only
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy exercise 24 hours before
  • Arrive 20 minutes early for check-in
  • Bring valid ID and medical documents
  • Book your appointment in advance
  • Estimated time: 2-3 hours for complete package
