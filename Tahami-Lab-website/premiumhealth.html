Recommended For

Preparation Guidelines

Before Your Test

  • 8-10 hours fasting (for accurate blood work)
  • Avoid strenuous exercise 24 hours before
  • Stay hydrated with water only
  • Wear loose-sleeved clothing
  • Arrive 10-15 minutes early
  • Bring valid ID and any medical documents
  • Continue regular medications unless advised otherwise
