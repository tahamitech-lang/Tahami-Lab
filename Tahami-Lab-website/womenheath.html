Specialized package for women's wellness

What's Included in Women's Health Package?

The Women's Health Package is a specialized comprehensive health screening designed specifically for women's wellness. It includes essential blood tests, thyroid screening, and specialized gynecological imaging tailored to address women's unique health needs and concerns.

🩸 Basic Blood Work Tests

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Blood Sugar (Glucose) Test
  • Blood Pressure Check
  • Liver Function Tests
  • Kidney Function Tests

🧬 Thyroid & Metabolic Screening

  • Thyroid Profile (TSH, T3, T4)
  • Lipid Profile (Cholesterol)

🔍 Women-Specific Imaging & Tests

  • Gynecological Ultrasound (Pelvic Ultrasound)
  • Hormone Tests (Estrogen, Progesterone)

What Each Section Covers

🩸 Blood Work - What It Detects

  • Anemia and blood-related issues (common in women)
  • Blood sugar and diabetes risk
  • Blood pressure and cardiovascular health
  • Liver and kidney function
  • Overall nutritional status

🧬 Thyroid & Hormonal Health

  • Thyroid function (very common issues in women)
  • Hormone imbalances
  • Cholesterol levels (heart disease risk)
  • Metabolic health assessment
  • Menopause and reproductive health screening

🔍 Gynecological Imaging & Hormones

  • Pelvic ultrasound (uterus, ovaries, fallopian tubes)
  • Detects cysts, fibroids, and abnormalities
  • Hormone level assessment
  • Reproductive health evaluation
  • Fertility assessment if needed

Who Should Get Women's Health Package?

Recommended For

  • All adult women for annual health checkup
  • Women experiencing hormonal changes
  • Pre-menopausal and menopausal women
  • Women with irregular menstrual cycles
  • Those planning pregnancy (fertility assessment)
  • Family history of women's health issues
  • Women above 30 years for preventive health
  • Anyone concerned about reproductive health

Common Women's Health Issues Detected

🩺 Conditions This Package Screens

  • Anemia: Very common in women (iron deficiency)
  • Thyroid Disorders: Women 5-8x more likely to have thyroid issues
  • Ovarian Cysts: Detected through pelvic ultrasound
  • Uterine Fibroids: Non-cancerous growths in uterus
  • Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS): Common hormone disorder
  • Endometriosis: Tissue growth outside uterus
  • Hormonal Imbalances: Identified through hormone testing
  • Cholesterol Issues: Cardiovascular risk assessment

Preparation Guidelines

Before Your Test

  • 8-10 hours fasting (for accurate blood work)
  • Wear loose-fitting comfortable clothing
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy exercise
  • Schedule ultrasound with full bladder if possible
  • Bring valid ID and any medical documents
  • Inform technician of any gynecological concerns
  • Arrive 15 minutes early for check-in
  • Estimated time: 1.5-2 hours for complete package
