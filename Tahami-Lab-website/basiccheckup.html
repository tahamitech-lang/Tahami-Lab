What Each Test Shows

🩸 CBC Test - What It Detects

  • Blood infections (bacterial, viral, fungal)
  • Anemia and blood deficiency
  • Immune system strength
  • Leukemia and blood disorders
  • Bleeding disorders and platelet issues

🍬 Blood Sugar Test - What It Detects

  • Diabetes diagnosis
  • Pre-diabetes (early warning)
  • Glucose metabolism problems
  • Risk of metabolic syndrome
  • Medication effectiveness monitoring

💓 Blood Pressure Check - What It Detects

  • Hypertension (high blood pressure)
  • Hypotension (low blood pressure)
  • Heart disease risk
  • Stroke risk assessment
  • Kidney disease indicators

Who Should Get Basic Checkup?

Recommended For

  • Annual routine health checkup
  • Age 18 and above
  • No specific health concerns
  • Budget-conscious individuals
  • First-time health screening
  • Regular health monitoring
  • Before starting new medication

Preparation Guidelines

Before Your Test

  • 8-10 hours fasting (for accurate results)
  • Drink water only before test
  • Wear loose-sleeved clothing
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy exercise
  • Arrive 10 minutes early
  • Bring valid ID
  • Sit and relax for 5 minutes before BP check
