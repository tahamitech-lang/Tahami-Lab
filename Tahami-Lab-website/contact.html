Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
+
You may book an appointment by calling +92 (300) 123-4567, emailing appointments@medicare.com, or using the contact form above. We also offer walk-in visits and home collection. Our team will confirm your booking within 24 hours and provide any preparation instructions.
What is the turnaround time for test results?
+
Routine blood test results are typically available within 24 hours. Specialized pathology tests may require 3–5 working days. Imaging reports are usually delivered the same day or within 24 hours. You will receive an email and SMS notification when your report is ready.
Do you offer home sample collection?
+
Yes. We provide professional home sample collection for most tests. To schedule, please provide your preferred date, time and address when booking. Our certified phlebotomists follow strict safety and infection-control protocols.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept cash, major credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile wallets (EasyPaisa, JazzCash). For corporate clients we offer tailored invoicing and payment terms—please contact our corporate services team for arrangements.
Can I access my reports online?
+
Yes. When your tests are complete you will receive an email with a secure link to access your report via our patient portal. If you need assistance accessing your report, contact support@medicare.com.
